# Remote Job Hunting

## Overview
Find international remote developer jobs paying $20-120/hr. AI training roles are the best entry point for technical workers in lower-cost regions.

## AI Training Platforms (Priority Order)
1. **Alignerr** ($40-120/hr) — AI training and evaluation. Highest pay.
2. **DataAnnotation** ($20-30/hr) — Consistent work, quick onboarding.
3. **Outlier** ($15-40/hr) — Various AI training projects.
4. **Scale AI** ($20-50/hr) — Enterprise AI data work.
5. **Telus International** ($12-20/hr) — Steady volume.
6. **Appen** ($10-20/hr) — Lower pay but consistent.

## Traditional Dev Platforms
- **LinkedIn Jobs** — Filter: Remote, Worldwide. Use Easy Apply.
- **We Work Remotely** — Strictly remote jobs. $50-150k range.
- **Wellfound** (AngelList) — Startup jobs. DM the founder directly.
- **RemoteOK** — Aggregator, good for discovery.
- **Turing** — Vetted developers, long-term contracts.

## Portfolio Strategy
1. Build 3 real projects (not tutorials)
2. Deploy them (live URLs matter)
3. Write case studies (problem → approach → result)
4. Open source contributions count more than side projects

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't apply to every job (quality over quantity)
- Don't use generic cover letters (mention a specific project)
- Don't undervalue yourself (AI training pays well globally)
